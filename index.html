Overview:

A project that aims to enhance accountability in local government by providing a platform for citizens to evaluate and rate elected officials based on their performance and service delivery.

FarmLink
Overview:

This project aims to revolutionize the operational efficiency of a company specializing in packaging and distributing organic vegetables grown by grassroots farmers in Cape Town townships. By implementing a robust system, it seeks to streamline farmer management, optimize route planning for daily collections and deliveries, and enhance overall business operations.


Purpose:

To replace manual and error-prone process with a comprehensive digital solution that integrates farmer management, route planning, and real-time data visualization. This system will enable the company to improve productivity, reduce costs, and meet customer demand more effectively


Audience:

The system targets business owners who manage operations, plan routes, and oversee logistics. Additionally, it serves contracted farmers who benefit from streamlined information access and optimized route schedules


Completed: 15-June-2024

Tech Stack: Backend: Node.js and Express, Database: MongoDB with Mongoose. Frontend: React, Google Maps for route visualization. Authentication: JWT tokens. Deployment: Render(Backend), Netlify(Frontend)
